Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most effective ways to prevent cancer?

  • Avoid tobacco use.
  • Maintain a healthy weight.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables.
  • Limit alcohol consumption.
  • Protect your skin from UV rays.
  • Stay physically active.

What are the early signs of breast cancer?

  • A lump or thickening in the breast or underarm.
  • Changes in breast size, shape, or appearance.
  • Nipple discharge (not breast milk).
  • Redness or scaling of the skin around the nipple or breast.

What are effective methods to quit smoking?

  • Use nicotine replacement therapies (patches, gum, lozenges).
  • Try prescription medications like varenicline or bupropion.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables.
  • Limit alcohol consumption.
  • Protect your skin from UV rays.
  • Stay physically active.

How can I manage stress effectively?

  • Exercise regularly.
  • Try prescription medications like varenicline or bupropion.
  • Stay connected with friends and family.
  • Seek professional help if needed.
