Our Mission

It’s easy to do great work when you believe in what you do. Velvom aims to prevent cancers where possible, with focus on healthy living, healthy eating and early screening. We aim to support management and affordability of cancer treatment.

Our Motivation

Cancer is one of the leading causes of illness and death in our country. Lack of awareness and absence of universal cancer screening results in late diagnosis. Women tend to delay their health issues and treatment for various reasons. All this leads to delay in diagnosis and treatment.

Our Story

Velvom, which means ‘we will win’ in Tamizh, was founded with the passion and vision to empower women about health and smart health choices, from adolescence to menopause and beyond. Velvom aims to increase awareness regarding cancers associated with women and tobacco related cancers, communicable diseases, and their prevention.

Our clients

Though started as a women’s health centre, our aim is preventive health and wellness. This is available to women and men alike. And tobacco users who want help to quit the habit.
