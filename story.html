Tobacco Cesssation Clinic

Oral cancer is a leading killer in women and men. Tobacco, esp smokeless kind being the leading cause. Most oral cancers are diagnosed late. Even those diagnosed early, the disfigurement caused by the minimal surgeries prevents patients from seeking help. So tobacco cessation and awareness becomes a priority. Velvom aspires to increase awareness about tobacco, and oral cancer. We provide an wholesome, evidence based tobacco cessation programme to help users break the habit. This combines specialist support, counselling and medication that is individualised. Screening and diagnoses of oral issues is offered, and timely treatment provided.

Mindful eating

Food nourishes body mind and soul. Putting our 100% to what we cook and what we eat depends on the following
-Age group -Stage of life (eg. pregnancy/ recovery from trauma )
-Profession you are in
-Lifestyle
As a rule our body requirements decrease when we grow beyond 30-35, thus decreasing our caloric requirements. So what should we put on our plates? What you need rather than what you want Eat food close to what your parents and grandparents ate with more local produce that is least processed. Eat when you need to eat not because you want to eat. Eating should not be resorted to for passing time rather the time spent eating should be spent well, with friends and family mindful of what goes in.
